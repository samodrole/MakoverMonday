Alcohol Consumption by Country

The highest consumption rates of alcohol seem to be concentrated in Europe and other places in the Northern Hemisphere of the globe. The highest rates can be seen in countries like Belarus, Lithuania, the Czech Republic, Ireland, and France. Meanwhile, countries in Asia, Africa, and the pacific have much lower rates of consumption.

Average consumption of alcohol within a country per capita consumption of pure alcohol in liters as a measurement to avoid being biased as much as possible.
